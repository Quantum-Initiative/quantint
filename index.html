Quantum Initiative

At Quantum Initiative, we explore the intersection of climate change and quantum mechanics. Our team explores unique ways to use quantum mechanical based models to study and tackle climate change from a novel data-analytical perspective, representing a novel method of data collection that could potentially have massive benefit to the scientific community.

Projects

Our first joint research project is underway! We are currently studying the applications of Quantum Monte Carlo--a quantum-based model--to simulate transition metal oxides, which are crucial compounds for reducing carbon dioxide emissions.
